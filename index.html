Breast Cancer Detection
Healthcare providers determine cancer types and subtypes so they can tailor treatment to be as effective as possible with the fewest possible side effects. Common types of breast cancer include:

Symptoms:

  • Lump in breast or underarm
  • Redness or thickening of nipple or skin
  • Early stage with few or no symptoms

Symptoms:

  • Persistent breast pain
  • Swelling in part of the breast
  • Nipple discharge (not milk)

Symptoms:

  • Thickening in breast
  • Dimpling or skin changes
  • Full/heavy feeling

Symptoms:

  • Fast-growing lump
  • Painless swelling
  • More common in younger women

Symptoms:

  • Red, warm, swollen breast
  • Pitted skin like orange peel
  • Rapid visible changes

Symptoms:

  • Rapid tumor growth
  • Aggressive symptoms
  • Responsive to targeted therapy
